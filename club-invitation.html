What it is

A collective, not a job.

The Headshot Club books professional headshot sessions by appointment. Clients choose a package and a time; a photographer from the roster shows up and shoots it. The club runs everything around the shoot, so you can focus on the part you love: being behind the camera.

The idea

You stay independent. You take the shoots that fit your schedule, do great work in the studio, and get paid a clear, fixed cut per session, no chasing clients, no invoicing, no editing backlog.

The vibe

Warm, professional, and well-run. We protect your time and your craft. A friendly client experience and consistent quality are what keep the collective booked.

How it works

We handle everything. You just shoot.

The club provides
  • All client communication & scheduling
  • Professional camera equipment
  • Lighting equipment & setups
  • The studio & location
  • All editing & final delivery
  • Payment & the booking system
You bring
  • Show up to the studio on time
  • Warm, professional communication with the client
  • Know Capture One (we'll teach you at orientation if you don't yet)
  • Follow the lighting protocol for the shoot type you're assigned

That's the whole deal. Everything operational is on us.

Getting shoots

How shoots are assigned.

1
First few months · while we build the roster

We ask first, always.

For each shoot, we check your availability ahead of time. You connect your calendar and block any dates you don't want to be considered for. You are never assigned a shoot you haven't accepted, if it doesn't work, it simply passes to someone else.

2
Once everyone is onboarded

First available takes it.

After the full roster is in place, new shoots go out to the group and the first available photographer claims it. Keeping your blocked dates current is all you need to do, you'll only ever see shoots you're free for.

Connecting your calendar and blocking your unavailable dates is required, it's what keeps assignments fair and stress-free.

If you're in

Headshot Day & orientation.

Accept below and you'll be invited to an in-person orientation. It's relaxed, about four hours, with snacks and drinks. You officially join the roster once you've attended.

  • We shoot your headshots. You leave with fresh professional images of yourself.
  • We create content together for the website and social media.
  • A short training session: how Capture One works, how to manage the client, and the lighting protocols.
  • Snacks, drinks, and good company. Meet the rest of the collective.
